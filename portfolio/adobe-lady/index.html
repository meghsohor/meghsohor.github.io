About Us

We are International graphic design outsource service provider, established in July 2012. We love compelling brands, products and service's, thats why we hand pick the clients we most admire and strive to form long lasting relationships with. A young and ambitious studio, Adobe lady has moved from strength to strength to work for clients globally on a range of scales. We love to attract forward thinking clients and tastemakers to produce design outcomes and collaborations that amplify existing brands and ensure that new start-ups are strategically thought out and break the moulds of their given sector.

Adobelady attains World Class service in graphics production. Our principal activity is Photoshop Path, Image Editing, Photoshop masking, Photoshop retouch, Photo Restoration, Color Correction, Typography, Panorama photography, Corporate Identity, Prepress, Animation Vector works, Logo Design, Brand Identity, Brochure Design, Flyer Design, Web Design and other design services. We are confident about our quality and fast pace service.
